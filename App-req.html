The Requirements

standard Requirements :

  • The student must be of Egyptian nationality.
  • The student must have completed the preparatory school certificate.
  • The student's age must not exceed 18 years by October 1, 2024.
  • The preparatory school certificate score must be at least 220, with a minimum score of 48 in English.
  • The student must pass admission tests in English, Mathematics, and Arabic, as well as a personal interview, career aptitude assessment, medical examination, and drug test.
